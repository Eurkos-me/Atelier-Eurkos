Well, hello there!

I am working on a web development course, learning how to build this website along the way. If you're seeing this, it probably looks drastically different than it did yesterday, and will likely look different again tomorrow.

Feel free to look around. There are a few pages to see, but most are likely going to still be fairly empty for now.

I've recently made a few changes to this layout, and I'm excited to show them off a bit.

  1. The layout is completely responsive. It will adjust itself depending on how large your device's display. The top navbar will morph to stack the components in a column instead of a row if the screen is too narrow to display them as a row.
  2. If you're using a mobile phone, the navbar will be on top while in portrait orientation, but will move to the left when turned to landscape orientation.
    • If your phone's screen is less than 600 pixels wide, it will not move the navbar to the left.
    • Instead, the navbar will scroll with the rest of the site content. You'll have to scroll back up to get to the navbar, but too bad. Get a new phone, my dude.
